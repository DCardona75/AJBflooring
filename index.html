AJB Flooring

At AJB Flooring, we are passionate about flooring. With many years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for providing high-quality flooring solutions to homeowners and businesses in the DFW area.

Our team of flooring experts takes the time to listen to your needs and preferences before making any recommendations. We believe in providing exceptional customer service from start to finish, with skilled installation and maintenance services at competitive prices. We offer a wide range of flooring options, including hardwood, laminate, tile, carpet, and vinyl, from some of the most trusted brands in the industry.

We believe in providing exceptional customer service from start to finish. Our skilled installation team uses only the best tools and materials to ensure a flawless installation every time. We also offer maintenance and repair services to keep your flooring looking beautiful for years to come.

Our Service

Tile

Our tile installation service provides a durable and low-maintenance flooring option for high-traffic areas such as kitchens and bathrooms. We offer a variety of tile options, including ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone, in a range of colors and designs to suit your style.

Carpet

Our carpet installation service provides a cozy and comfortable flooring option for bedrooms, living rooms, and other areas where you want a soft surface to walk on. We offer a range of carpet types, including plush, textured, and patterned, in a variety of colors and materials.

Hardwood

Our hardwood installation service provides a timeless and elegant flooring option that adds warmth and character to any room. We offer a range of hardwood species, including oak, maple, and cherry, in a variety of finishes and widths to suit your style.

Laminate

Our laminate installation service provides a cost-effective and low-maintenance flooring option that looks like real wood or tile. We offer a range of laminate options, including embossed, textured, and smooth finishes, in a variety of colors and patterns.

No matter which flooring option you choose, our team of experts will provide skilled installation and maintenance services to ensure that your new floors look beautiful for years to come. Contact us today to learn more about our services and to schedule a consultation.
